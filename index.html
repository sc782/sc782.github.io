Bio

Hi! I am a 1st-year PhD Student in Computer Science at University of Wisconsin-Madison advised by Prof. Fred Sala as part of Sprocket Lab. My current research interests broadly include topics in machine (un)learning, time-series forecasting, and geometric deep learning.

I received my M.S. in Computer Science from Cornell University under the supervision of Prof. David Bindel and Prof. David Mimno. I also received my B.A. from Cornell in Computer Science and Mathematics.
